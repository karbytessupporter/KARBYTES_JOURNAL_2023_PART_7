For approximately $160 I purchased a compact sleeping bag from the REI store near Warm Springs BART station. Then I attempted to rent a small mailbox at the UPS store in Castro Valley in order to receive my social security card as securely as possible (but my application for a mailbox was rejected due to the fact that my current driver’s license shows a different address than what is on the printout of one of my Patelco bank statements). My plan is to go to the Social Security Office in Fremont on Monday to order a new social security card and to have it sent to my dad’s house. Hopefully I get the SSC in time and bring it to the Ontrac office in time to have it submitted to Human Resources.
